A tool for measuring, visualizing, and connecting through the physics of sound.  Not a music player. Not a social app with dark mode. A precision instrument for sonic intelligence.
Identity

Modern Dark Cinema + Precision Lab Instrument.  The foundation is deep void black with glassmorphism and ambient light blooms — immersive, cinematic. The overlay is scientific data visualization: tabular floats, monospace measurements, region-of-interest highlighting. Not cyberpunk (too gimmicky). Not vaporwave (too retro).

Three accent domains with strict mapping: purple for neural/brain, cyan for audio/signal, green for pulse/haptic only. Never cross the streams. Every measurement reads like an fMRI readout, not a streaming player.
